Pratik Panels Ltd., formerly known as Raipur Panels Pvt Ltd., was incorporated in 1989 and was converted to a public ltd. Co in 1994.

The company has works and registered office in Raipur and corporate office at Mumbai. At Raipur works, the company has Veneer Mill, Sawmill, Plywood, and wood working manufacturing facilities, using most upto date technologies and machinery. Among the other equipment company uses machines like Cremona, Taihei and Marunaka Slicers, Ruckle doublecut Guiollotine, Jointer, Ruckle Crossfeed Splicer, Cremona jumping saw Etc for making best quality decorative veneers and plywood thereof.

Using the best available technology and employing knowledgeable and experienced technologist, the company is able to make best use of natural timber specialising in Burma Teak wood for its Teak Veneered products. Company makes its most popular Burma Teak Veneered plywood in two range namely Premium and Gold and in various thickness like  3.5, 4,6,9,12 & 18(mm) and in various sizes  244*122, 244*92, 214*122, 214*92, 184*122, 184*92, 154*122, 154*92(cms).

The Company is also involved in Exports of Burma Teak products like Veneer, Lumber, Decking, Parquets etc. used mostly in luxurious yatch, five star hotels and business centers. Our export business network extends to Europe, America and Asia.

The company has it’s own sawmill, profile 4 spindle moulder (S4S) and parquet making facilities.

                 Various products that company makes and standard of quality control combined with management system it employs has been awarded as  ISO: 9001 certified company.
