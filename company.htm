COMPANY INFO

Pratik Panels Ltd., formerly known as Raipur Panels Pvt Ltd., was incorporated in 1989 and was converted to a Public Limited Company in 1994 having its registered office at  44 & 56 Rawabhatta, Industrial Estate, Bilaspur Road, Raipur (C.G) 493 221 and Corporate Office at 1, Usha Niketan, Mahant Road, Ville Parle East, Mumbai- 400 057.

And the Company is listed at Bombay Stock Exchange, National Stock Exchange and Indore Stock Exchange.

The Company is in the field of Burma Teak business since last 20 Years. The Company is also involved in Exports of Burma Teak products like Veneer, Lumber, Decking, Decking Panels, Pre Fabricated Deckings, Parquets and Marine Products etc. used mostly in luxurious yacht, ships , five star hotels and business centers. Our export business network extends to Europe, America and Asia.

 Our Export Products : TEAK DECKING ,FLEXI DECK PANELS, PRE -FABRICATED DECKINGS, DECKING VENEERS, MINI DECKING , MARGIN & LUMBER.

Made from 100 % Quarter Sawn Burma Teakwood, Decking Grade Teakwood , without defect, Light Golden color.

 

PREMIUM QUALITY  MARINE PLYWOOD, ARCHITECTURAL PLYWOOD

DECORATIVE PLYWOOD, TEAK FACE UPTO 2.6 MM, MAHOGONY FACE

SPECIALITY PLYWOOD, HOLLY TEAK, FLEXI PLY, SANDWITCH PLYWOOD.

Made from 100 % KERUING OF BUREMESE ORIGIN, made with

ALL 1.00 MM VENEERS (COMPLETELY BALANCED).

 

Company is Exporting these Products to various Countries like France, Denmark, USA, Greece & Japan.                     

 Using the best available technology and employing knowledgeable and experienced technologist, the company is able to make best use of natural timber specializing in Burma Teak wood.  At our works, the company has Veneer Mill, Sawmill, CNC Router, Plywood, and wood working manufacturing facilities, using most up to date technologies and machinery. Among the other equipment company uses machines like Cremona, Taihei and Marunaka Slicers, Ruckle double cut Guillotine Jointer, Ruckle Cross feed Splicer, Cremona jumping saw, profile 4 spindle moulder (S4S) , Membrane Press Etc.
